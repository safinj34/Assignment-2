ALWAYS FRESH
FLOWERS

Fresh flowers are recently cut blooms that are vibrant, firm, and have a natural fragrance, lacking signs of wilting or browning. Key characteristics include sturdy, moist stems, firm green leaves and calyxes, and petals that are still bright. The quality of a flower can be assessed by its sturdy stems, firm petals, lack of damage, and vibrant, unfaded color.
